Conditions Treated

Our practice treats a variety of neurological conditions including the following:

  • ALS
  • Alzheimer's
  • Back pain
  • Bell's palsy
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Cephalic disorders
  • Chronic pain
  • Dementias
  • Guillain-Barre syndrome
  • Headaches/migraine
  • Hemifacial spasm
  • Huntington's disease (HD)
  • Infectious diseases
  • Movement disorders
  • Multiple sclerosis (MS)
  • Myasthenia gravis
  • Myopathies
  • Neuropathy
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Seizures (epilepsy)
  • Sleep disorders
  • Stroke
  • Tremor
  • Tumors (of the nervous system)
  • Vertigo

    • Diagnostic and Treatment Procedures Available

    We also perform a variety of diagnostics in our office including the following:

    • Botox injections for treating movements disorders and a variety of neurological conditions
    • Trigger point injections - targeted injection of anti-inflammatory agent and local anesthetic directly into affected muscle tissue for the purpose of pain relief.
    • EEG (electroencephalogram)
    • EMG/NCV studies (electromyography/nerve conduction)
    • Lumbar punctures
    • Tensilon testing
